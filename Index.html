Install as App
To install Lix on your phone or share with another teacher:
1. Open this file in Chrome
2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Lix appears on your launcher with the fleur-de-lis icon
Share the HTML file with any teacher — they follow the same steps on their own phone. Each person's data stays separate.
